## Overview

An AI-powered toolkit for analyzing African literature, combining computational linguistics with literary analysis to uncover patterns, themes, and stylistic features in African storytelling traditions.

## Features

- **Theme Detection**: Identifies recurring themes and motifs
- **Stylistic Analysis**: Analyzes writing style and literary devices
- **Character Network Analysis**: Maps character relationships
- **Cultural Context Recognition**: Identifies cultural references
- **Comparative Analysis**: Compares works across authors and periods

## Applications

Useful for literary scholars, students, and researchers studying African literature, enabling large-scale analysis of literary corpora and uncovering insights that would be difficult to identify manually.
