## Overview

A comprehensive digital archive dedicated to preserving African oral literature, folktales, and storytelling traditions using modern web technologies and AI-powered tools for analysis and categorization.

## Features

- **Story Database**: Searchable collection of African folktales
- **Multimedia Support**: Audio recordings of oral storytelling
- **AI Analysis**: Automatic theme and motif detection
- **Cultural Context**: Detailed annotations and cultural notes
- **Interactive Maps**: Geographic origins of stories

## Impact

Preserving cultural heritage through technology, making African storytelling traditions accessible to global audiences while maintaining cultural authenticity.
