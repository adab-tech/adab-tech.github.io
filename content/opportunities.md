# Opportunities

This page is dedicated to sharing scholarships, admissions, fellowships, internships, and related opportunities for the general public, with a special focus on empowering younger individuals pursuing careers in languages, technology, AI, and digital humanities.

---

## Why This Page?

Access to information about opportunities can be transformative, especially for students and early-career professionals in Africa and other underrepresented regions. This curated list aims to:

- **Democratize access** to educational and professional opportunities
- **Empower the next generation** of language technologists and digital humanists
- **Build community** by sharing resources that have helped others
- **Support African students** and researchers in accessing global opportunities

---

## How to Use This Page

- **Check back regularly** - This page will be updated with new opportunities as they become available
- **Act quickly** - Many opportunities have limited application windows
- **Read requirements carefully** - Make sure you meet eligibility criteria before applying
- **Prepare in advance** - Keep your CV, transcripts, and recommendation letters ready
- **Don't self-reject** - If you meet most requirements, apply anyway!

## Tips for Success

### For Scholarship Applications
1. **Start early** - Give yourself at least 2-3 months
2. **Tailor your application** - Customize for each opportunity
3. **Show impact** - Highlight how the opportunity will help you make a difference
4. **Get strong recommendations** - Choose recommenders who know your work well
5. **Proofread everything** - Errors can disqualify otherwise strong applications

### For Internships & Jobs
1. **Build a portfolio** - Showcase your projects on GitHub
2. **Network actively** - Attend conferences and join online communities
3. **Learn continuously** - Take online courses and certifications
4. **Contribute to open source** - Demonstrates skills and collaboration
5. **Practice interviews** - Prepare for technical and behavioral questions

### General Advice
- **Stay organized** - Track deadlines and requirements in a spreadsheet
- **Ask for help** - Reach out to mentors and previous recipients
- **Be persistent** - Rejection is common; keep applying
- **Celebrate small wins** - Each application is progress
- **Pay it forward** - When you succeed, help others
