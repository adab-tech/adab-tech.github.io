# Services

I offer a range of professional services at the intersection of languages, literature, and artificial intelligence. Whether you're looking to build language technology solutions, analyze textual data, or develop educational platforms, I bring expertise in both the technical and linguistic aspects of these challenges.

## Language Technology Consulting

**Natural Language Processing (NLP) Solutions**
- Custom NLP models for low-resource languages
- Multilingual sentiment analysis
- Machine translation systems
- Text classification and named entity recognition
- Language model fine-tuning and adaptation

**African Language Technology**
- Hausa language processing and applications
- Digital resources for underrepresented African languages
- Cross-lingual transfer learning
- Language documentation and corpus development

## Digital Humanities & Literary Analysis

**Computational Literary Analysis**
- Digital text analysis and visualization
- Topic modeling and thematic analysis
- Stylometric analysis and authorship attribution
- Digital archives and cultural heritage projects
- Interactive literary exploration tools

**Research Support**
- Data collection and corpus building
- Statistical analysis of textual data
- Research methodology consulting
- Academic writing and publication support

## Educational Technology

**Language Learning Applications**
- Interactive language learning platforms

**Content Development**
- Educational content creation
- Curriculum design for language and AI courses

- Multimedia educational resources

## AI & Machine Learning Solutions

**Custom AI Applications**

**Technical Consulting**

## Speaking & Workshops

**Presentations**

- Webinars on AI and digital humanities
