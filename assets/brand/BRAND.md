# Adamu Abubakar — Brand identity (adamu.tech)

Print-ready vector assets for stickers, business cards, letterhead, and merchandise.

## Logos

| File | Use |
|------|-----|
| `logo-mark.svg` | App icon, navbar, social avatars (512×512 artboard) |
| `logo-horizontal.svg` | Website header, email signatures, wide layouts |
| `logo-print.svg` | Print vendors (1200×1200 with safe margin) |
| `favicon.svg` | Browser tab / PWA |

## Colors

| Name | Hex | Role |
|------|-----|------|
| Teal | `#0f766e` | Primary accent |
| Indigo | `#4f46e5` | Secondary accent |
| Gold | `#bb6b1f` | Warm highlight |
| Ink | `#102036` | Text / dark UI |
| Paper | `#f6f1e8` | Background |

**Print (approximate CMYK):** Teal C72 M18 Y58 K42 · Indigo C78 M72 Y0 K0 · Gold C8 M48 Y88 K12

## Typography

- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (600–700)
- **Body:** Inter (400–600)

## Clear space

Keep padding around the mark equal to **25% of the mark height** (no text or busy graphics inside).

## Minimum size

- Screen: 32px height  
- Print: 12mm (0.47 in) mark height  

## Booking calendar

Set your Cal.com link in `assets/brand/site-config.js` (`bookingCalLink` and `bookingUrl`).

## Shipping to print

Export from `logo-print.svg` as PDF or PNG at **300 DPI**. Ask the vendor for: matte vinyl sticker, 2×2 in or 3×3 in, or business card 3.5×2 in with mark on one side.
