About Me

I am a dedicated PhD candidate specializing in Francophone African Literature, with a deep passion for language, culture, and technology. My academic journey has equipped me with a unique blend of expertise in literary analysis, multilingual communication, and digital innovation.

As a multilingual AI trainer, I work on cutting-edge NLP projects, helping to build more inclusive and accurate language models. My expertise spans across Hausa, English, and French, enabling me to bridge cultural and linguistic gaps in technology and research.

Beyond academia, I am passionate about digital storytelling, technical writing, and creating educational resources that make complex topics accessible to diverse audiences. I believe in the power of technology to preserve cultural heritage while driving innovation.

When I'm not immersed in research or AI training, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing insights through writing and teaching.
